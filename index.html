About Us

AIT Pune

  • Army Institute of Technology, Pune, a premier engineering college affiliated with the Savitribai Phule Pune University, was established in India in 1994.
  • It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and is run by the Army Welfare Education Society (AWES).
  • The institute is primarily meant for the wards of serving and retired army personnel.

DDQ

  • The Debating, Dramatics, and Quizzing Club, commonly known as the DDQ club, is a dynamic community of students.
  • The club recognizes the importance of developing leadership skills and encourages students to actively organise and execute events.
  • By participating in our club’s activities, students are equipped with the tools and confidence needed to make a meaningful impact in their communities and beyond.

AIT MUN

  • The main goal of a Model United Nations (MUN) conference is to facilitate the development of essential real-life skills such as public speaking, negotiation, and problem-solving for participants who believe in their own capabilities.
  • MUN events are not mere imitations of real UN conferences, as they require quick thinking on one's feet and aim to prepare tomorrow's leaders.
  • In India, MUNs have become a flagship event for any literary association, offering a unique experience for each participant.

Commitee

UNHRC (United Nations Human Rights Council): This committee will allow the delegates to raise concerns over the rights of inmates and detainees under international human rights law and human rights issues such as eradicating prejudice against immigrants based on race, gender with special focus on Uyghur people in China.
DISEC (Disarmament and International Security Committee): It has been adopted as the first of six General Assembly committees because of its essential role in maintaining international peace and security and aims at deliberation over the Disarmament of unlawful weapons in the Russia-Ukraine war.
G20 : India holds the G20 presidency and its motto is "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam” or “One Earth · One Family". The G20 group aims at a discussion on collective action for restoring global economic and financial stability in the aftermath of the pandemic.
International Press (IP):To resound the motto of a free, independent press that is devoid of prejudice and ill will. There are two things which come as a part of International Press- (1) Journalism and (2) Photography.
